What support should I expect?

Typically, we're looking for founders with a product in market and early traction. If you are at that stage and are ready to leverage our network and expert team, now's the right time.
What are the terms?

We'll build you a 6-month bespoke program, invest 30k-100k in capital and take 4%-7% equity, depending on your startup’s current valuation. *

*Our program and offering differs in London, Paris, New York and Johannesburg.
Does Evermore only invest in certain sectors?

We'll build you a 6-month bespoke program, invest 30k-100k in capital and take 4%-7% equity, depending on your startup’s current valuation. * *Our program and offering differs in London, Paris, New York and Johannesburg.
Will you build my product?

When it comes to product, we believe that short term development resource is unsustainable. We'll work with you on your architecture, will help you review code, and will coach your team to deliver a better product. All of which will give you learnings you can take with you after the program.
What happens when the program is finished?

While formal meetings will pause, you'll continue to leverage our global alumni network and knowledge.
Can you guarantee me a corporate partnership?

At the very least, we'll help you build relationships with individuals within our partner's organisations, but there is no guarantee of a partnership materialising during the 6-month program. We’re proud of our record so far, but on both sides, people, strategy and objectives can change so it’s not something we can guarantee.
Do the corporates have any rights to my business?

They don't. If a partnership forms with one of our corporate partners, it will be governed by the usual pilot or contract agreements between you and them all of which our team can help with.
